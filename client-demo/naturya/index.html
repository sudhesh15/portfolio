About US

NATURYA, a brand owned by ARUSHIAN KEMMANNU FOODS and Beverages LLP, is a distinguished company based in Karnataka, India, specializing in the manufacturing of diverse FMCG food products. Our unwavering vision is to safeguard our current business by taking the lead in sales, quality, and innovation across all channels within our core categories.

Driven by our mission, we strive to fortify our company by strengthening our brand presence and continuously developing new cooking essentials. As we aim for growth, our ambition extends to expanding our reach nationwide and venturing into the international market, bringing the essence of NATURYA to discerning consumers globally.
